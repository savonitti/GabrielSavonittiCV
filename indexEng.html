About

My Journey so far

Fascinated by games, creation and legos since my childhood I played and created a lot, for my education I tried to learn more about games, being very interested in animation, character creation and world creation.

With the assignments and the Undergraduate thesis, I ended up getting closer to programming and enjoying seeing the code bring games to life, learning C# and Unity for this purpose.

Using my self-taught English, I started teaching and translating documents in English at first as an extra income, but I took a liking to teaching, and that's how I ventured with my master's and doctorate, obtaining scholarships at both PUC and later at USP with the support of CAPES.

Nowadays I work as a freelancer in the area of translations and Front-End development, combining my skills and creativity for solving problems, automating activities and creating differentiated content, paying attention to the needs and taste of my target audience.
